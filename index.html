Introduction


   Welcome to Lilybrary, a vibrant community space where knowledge and imagination come alive. Our collection features a diverse array of books, e-books, and multimedia resources, catering to all ages and interests. Whether you are a student, researcher, or avid reader, our library provides a tranquil environment and state-of-the-art facilities to support your intellectual journey.


  Explore our extensive catalog, join in on engaging events, or simply enjoy a quiet moment of reading in our serene spaces. We are dedicated to fostering a love of learning and promoting a culture of curiosity and discovery. Our friendly staff are always here to help you find exactly what you need and make your visit a memorable one.


  Firstly, there are services and facilities that surely will satisfy our customers such as references services, livechat, discussion room, 24/7 room and bookdrop. Next, there are few collections in our library such as books, e-books, arts and exhibition along with special collection such as manuscripts and old rare books. Lastly, Lilybrary also provides activities and programs that could give benefits For instance, workshop such as for ICT, book club, board games as well as campaigns such as HPV awareness.


Join us and become part of a community that celebrates the joy of reading, learning, and growing together!
