Books

E-books

Our e-book collection offers the convenience of accessing a vast library of titles from the comfort of your home or on-the-go. With genres ranging from fiction and non-fiction to academic texts and bestsellers, there is always something for every reader.




Special Collection

Our special collection is a treasure trove of unique and rare materials that highlight the rich cultural and historical heritage of our community. This collection includes:


  • Local History and Genealogy: Documents, photographs, and records that trace the history and ancestry of our region.
  • Rare Books and Manuscripts: Unique items that offer a glimpse into the past, including first editions and historical manuscripts.
