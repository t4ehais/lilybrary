Services & Facilities

References Services

Known as References Services, where users can make inquiries and seek advice from the Reference Librarian in all aspects of information such as library collection, tools, databases, services, facilities and others at reader’s advisory desk. Usually, the materials requested will take 2 to 3 working days depends on the research. Nonetheless, our librarians will teach how to use digital references service that offered so users can just retrieve online.




Discussion Room

The rooms can be utilized for group discussions or meetings. There are 5 such rooms located on the Ground and First Level of the Lilybrary. These rooms can vbe occupied up to 5 to 10 people. There are also whiteboards, markers, power source, plug extension and projector provided. Make sure to book the room beforehand as it is always fully occupied.




24/7 Room

Located outside of the Lilybrary building, the 24-hours Room is equipped with power source and wireless Internet facilities (WIFI). The study area is more comfortable with carrels provided. Additional equipments that are provided are sofas, reading material shelves and glass walls that replaced whiteboards for learning and discussion purposes.




Book Drop

Another advanced service provided by Lilybrary is the Book Drop service. It is a self-return service located on the left side of the library’s main entrance which allows borrowers to return all materials at their own convenient time. When the material is dropped, its Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) tag is instantly reactivated to register the return date of the borrowed material. This offers convenience of returning library items at anytime of the day, even when the library is closed.




Livechat

Live Chat is a platform for direct interaction between users and the librarian. Through these applications, users can interact with Lilybrary's librarians who are specially assigned to answer any relevant questions from users. Users also request the materials they asked to be sent or retrieved online through this livechat.
