Activities




Book Club ICT Workshop Board Games HPV Awareness Campaign



Book Club

Join us at the library every month for our book club and discover the joy of reading! Readers of all ages and interests are invited to join our group, whether you're an avid reader or just want to explore fresh books. Take part in exciting debates, express your opinions, and read a variety of genres. In a welcoming atmosphere, make new friends to improve your reading experience.




ICT Workshop

Come to the library for a fun ICT workshop with us! Our programme covers important digital skills, from basic computer operations to safe online navigation, whether you have no experience or wanting to extend your knowledge. Gain knowledge in a relaxed environment with hands-on learning and guidance from professionals.




Board Games

Play board games at the library for a moment to release stress and unwind! Come play games with other players of both classic and modern titles. There is something for everyone, regardless of experience scale from casual players to seasoned players. In a laid-back and social setting, enjoy a range of games with your friends or make new ones.




HPV Awareness Campaign

Come to the library with us for an educational HPV awareness session. Find more about the risks of the human papillomavirus (HPV), the advantages of early detection, and vaccination. Specialists will answer questions, share valuable knowledge, and recommend solutions to keep you well-informed.
